    Financial Health for Millenials

    Frosty Finance

    Frosty Finance is a web app that helps those without good financial health and knowledge to begin forming habits to save money and pay down debts. Aimed at millennials, Frosty Finance helps users to take control of their finances by focusing on short-term savings and debt reduction. Foregoing all-in-one budgeting, the focus is on achievable goals over the short term that build momentum through time. Focusing on the snowflake method of savings and the debt snowball method of loan repayment, Frosty Finance is a web app that focused on behavioural psycology to build good habits

    Mobile Bible App

    Decha

    A mobile reading app, Decha is based on a unique method of reading through the bible. Most reading plans focus on a linear approach to reading the entirety of scripture. Using Professor Grant Horner's Bible reading system, Decha allows for this to be used with a mobile bible instead of relying on a physical Bible and bookmarks that are not always convenint to have with you. Instead of needing a notebook to try and keep track of where you are, Decha is focused on keeping track of where you are in each of the reading lists, allowing Bible reading to be done whenver you have a spare few minutes and your phone.

    My Process

    UX Design is incredibly important for any digital effort. It ensures that the right approcah, the right people, and the right resources are all being used for any given product. All UX starts with the right questions.

    • Who are we building this for?
    • What are we building?
    • How are we going to build it?
    • Why are we building this for these users?

    The best way to answer all of these questions is through research

    User Research

    The first steps of any project should be research. After recieving the design brief the users for this product need to be identified, along with key information about their demographics, goals, interested, level of technical experience, and potential budget. There should also be a round of research conducted on competing products as well to see what are commoon paradigms, what are they doing righ, and what they are doing wrong. This allows for differentiation within the market, along with building a product that serves the users better than any other product.

    Personas

    While not actual users, Personas put a face and a name to users, allowing a design to be held up to an archetypical user. Design designs can be assessed base on whether or not they will serve these examples, and are a constant reminder that all development should happen with the end user in mind and not in a vacuum.
