Who I Am

I am a designer passionate about creating functional and effective web sites, print pieces, & graphics.

I was born and raised in sunny, swampy Central Florida. I received my two AS degrees from Valencia College in Central Florida, one focused on Print Design & the other on Interaction Design. I worked among the palmettos, pine trees, and alligators for the first four years after graduation, handling design for both print and digital projects.

After spending most of my life in the heat humidity I trecked north looking for colder weather. I’m currently drifting northward towards the Arctic at a steady pace, enjoying the change of seasons as I go. I love doing interactive and digital work, creating design that solves real problems for both users and businesses. If you need UI/UX work, web design, or graphic design feel free to contact me. Bonus points if you live in a cool climate. I look forward to talking to you.

What I Do

I work in both the print and digital realms to create the designs that you need to improve your business.

All design is aimed at one goal, making your business better. What kind of design is the thing that changes between each project. Wether that is on paper or in pixels, I can help you to reach your customers where ever they're at.

Brand Identity

Who you are and what your customers know about you is one of the most important things for a business to do well. I can work with you in figuring out who you're ideal customers are and how to design your brand to speak directly to them with you Brand. After all your brand is the signature for your business.

Illustration

Growing up down the road from Disney World I've been drawing as long as I can remember. Creating unique Illustrations is one of the best ways to make your business stand out. I'm always thrilled when I can make good art that increases your bottom line.

Web Design

A web presence is crucial to the succes of any business in this digital age. Your website is the center of your digital presence. A well planned and desinged web site gives your control of how your customers find & learn about your business.

Print Design

I come from a print background, having slung ink for my first internship screen printing posters. I still love laying out text, working up illustrations, and designing logos. The goal is always to serve the need of the project, whether that be an interface that allows for easier use of a checkout system, or a series of posters to raise brand recognition in the local area.

UI/UX Design

UX Design is incredibly important for any web project. It ensures that the right approcah, the right people, and the right resources are being maximized to reach your customers. Good UI Design is how we take all of that planning and put it into code so that your customers have a seemless experience when dealing with your business.

SEO

A good website can only take you so far. Your customers need to be able to find you, and optimizing your web presence to show up in search is the best way to do that. I can help you find the right words & strategies to bring your customers to your website. Your business is helps your customers. Let me help them find it.

Camp Hope

Web Design

Project Link

Camp Hope is a summer camp located in the foothills of the smoky mountains. A non-denominational church camp, its main focus is its summer season, consisting of week-long camps that are geared to specific ages and groups. Started in the 1970’s, Camp had a website, but one about 10 years out of date that was maintained by an overworked camp administrator who did not come from a web background. This redesign revamped the information architecture and setup a CMS for ease in maintaining and updating.
